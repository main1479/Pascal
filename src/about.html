vision

Helping Advisors Take Back Their Time

From compliance to competitors, wealth advisors are facing a growing list of challenges. Technology shouldn’t be one of them.

That’s why we believe implementing and using digital technology should be effortless. Well-designed technology gives advisors back the time and energy they need to engage with their clients, grow their business and pursue the things that matter most in their lives.

We’re achieving this vision with a highly intuitive wealthtech platform that comprehensively integrates key components of the wealth management practice. Leveraging best-in-class user design, Pascal delivers an end-to-end digital platform that enhances client-advisor engagement to increase share of wallet and referrals, streamlines compliance for greater efficiencies and lower admin costs, and utilizes intelligent algorithms for better investment outcomes to increase client trust and retention.
